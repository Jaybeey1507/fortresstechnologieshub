What is Fortress HPCX?

Fortress HPCX is a high-performance computing (HPC) optimization engine that integrates directly into SLURM, Kubernetes, and other platforms to monitor resource metrics, detect inefficiencies, and automatically apply smart fixes. HPCX gives visibility into system performance, predicts future bottlenecks, and resolves issues in real time — so you can scale with confidence.

Core Features

Metrics Monitoring

Live tracking of CPU, RAM, power, and job queues with historical view.

Job Analytics

Intelligent profiling of job delays, energy usage, and performance behavior.

Optimization Snapshots

Capture before/after states to measure impact of fixes and track improvement.

Security Alerts

Detect compliance violations, malicious activity, or abnormal system behavior.

Health Score

Quantitative rating of overall system performance, updated continuously.

Forecast Report

AI-powered predictions of energy spikes, bottlenecks, and queue buildup.

SIEM Integration

Send logs and alerts to your existing SIEM for unified security monitoring.

Fix Capabilities

Job Rescheduling

Reprioritize and shift jobs to eliminate unnecessary delays.

Job Rerouting

Dynamically reroute jobs to less congested nodes or clusters.

Job Throttling

Apply smart throttling to avoid thermal spikes and energy overloads.

Smart Suggestions

Get actionable insights and recommendations tailored to your system.

Green Mode

Eco-friendly scheduling that reduces energy usage during peak load.

Who Should Use HPCX?

Fortress HPCX is designed for government agencies, research institutes, universities, data centers, cloud infrastructure teams, and enterprise clusters. If your environment runs hundreds to thousands of batch jobs daily and performance matters — HPCX will make your system smarter, faster, and more sustainable.
