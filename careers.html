Independent Sales Partner
Independent Contractor 🌍 Remote, Canada-Wide Actively Recruiting

We are looking for motivated, professional, and well-connected individuals across Canada to join our Sales Partner Program for API Fortress, our enterprise API security platform. This is a commission-only independent contractor role with no ceiling on earnings. You bring the clients, we handle everything else, and you earn a recurring monthly commission for as long as each client stays active. The more clients you build in your portfolio, the more you earn every single month without doing additional work.

~30%
Recurring commission rate across all tiers
Up to $1,499
Per active client per month

What you will be doing

Identify and reach out to businesses, startups, and enterprises that could benefit from API Fortress, our AI-powered API security platform.
Present the product, answer initial questions using our provided pitch materials, and bring prospects to the point of signing up.
Earn a recurring monthly commission on every active paying client you onboard, for as long as they remain subscribed. Starter clients pay $699/month and you earn $209. Professional clients pay $1,999/month and you earn $599. Enterprise clients pay $4,999/month and you earn $1,499. Every month. As long as they stay.
Work fully remotely on your own schedule with no fixed hours, no quotas, and complete control over your approach.
Build a growing portfolio of active clients so your monthly earnings compound over time without proportional extra effort.

Who we are looking for

Someone with a background in B2B sales, business development, account management, or a strong professional network in the tech, fintech, healthcare, or enterprise space.
A self-starter who does not need hand-holding and understands how to identify decision-makers and have credible conversations with CTOs, IT directors, and heads of engineering.
Someone based in Canada who takes this seriously as a genuine income opportunity, not a side experiment.
Honest, professional, and comfortable representing a technology brand with integrity.
