Jubril Akanbi

Founder & CEO, Fortress Technologies Hub

From the historic town of Iwo in Osun State, Nigeria, to the tech corridors of Toronto, Canada, my journey has been one of relentless curiosity and purposeful innovation.

I earned my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology in 2022, where my passion for building impactful software first took root. Seeking to push my boundaries further, I chose Canada for my advanced studies, drawn by its world-class education, vibrant diversity, and culture of inclusivity that welcomes dreamers from every corner of the globe.

Now completing my Master of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University Vancouver, I founded Fortress Technologies Hub with a singular mission: to build technology that genuinely matters. Not technology for technology's sake, but solutions that protect, empower, and elevate what humans can achieve.
