Canadian Data Sovereignty

Your Data Stays in Canada

Most AI companies are American. That means your data, your conversations, and your business information are subject to the US CLOUD Act, which allows the US government to compel American companies to hand over data stored anywhere in the world.

At Fortress Technologies Hub, we made a different choice. CoreMind runs entirely on Canadian-owned infrastructure hosted in Montreal, Quebec. Your data never crosses the border. It is never subject to foreign surveillance laws. It is yours.

🏗️

Canadian-Owned Infrastructure

CoreMind runs on Patrii Cloud, a Canadian-owned server provider based in Montreal, Quebec. Not AWS. Not Google Cloud. Not Azure.

🚫

No CLOUD Act Exposure

Because we are a Canadian company using Canadian infrastructure, your data is not subject to the US CLOUD Act or other foreign surveillance legislation.

🔐

Self-Hosted AI Models

Our core AI models run on our own servers. Your conversations are not sent to third-party American AI providers for processing.

🇨🇦

Built for Canadian Businesses

Designed with Canadian privacy standards in mind, giving organizations the confidence that their data is handled responsibly and locally.
