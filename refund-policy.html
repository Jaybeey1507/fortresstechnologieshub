Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Our Commitment: We want you to be satisfied with our services. If you're not happy, we offer fair refund options depending on the product and circumstances.

1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and cancellations for products and services offered by Fortress Technologies Hub Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"). This policy applies to our consumer product CoreMind and our enterprise product API Fortress.

2. CoreMind Refund Policy

2.1 Subscription Plans

CoreMind offers various subscription plans. Our refund policy for subscriptions is as follows:

Request Timing Refund Amount Conditions
Within 7 days of initial purchase Full refund (100%) No questions asked
8-14 days after purchase 50% refund Must provide reason
After 14 days No refund May cancel for future billing

2.2 Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscription plans:

  • Within 14 days: Full refund available
  • After 14 days: Pro-rated refund for unused months may be available at our discretion
  • After 3 months: No refund, but you may cancel to prevent future renewal

If you signed up for a free trial and were subsequently charged, you may request a full refund within 7 days of being charged, provided you did not make significant use of the paid features during that period.

3. API Fortress Refund Policy

3.1 Enterprise Subscriptions

API Fortress is an enterprise product with customized pricing and terms. Refund policies for API Fortress are governed by your individual service agreement. Generally:

If you are participating in a pilot or trial program for API Fortress, the terms of that program will specify any applicable refund policies.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Services that have been fully delivered or consumed
  • Accounts terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service
  • Charges older than 90 days
  • Third-party fees or taxes
  • Custom development or consulting services already performed

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Email us at billing@fortresstechnologieshub.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email, order/transaction ID, and reason for the refund request
  3. Wait for our response within 2-3 business days

In some cases, we may offer service credits instead of monetary refunds. Service credits:

8. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

We understand that special circumstances may arise. If you believe your situation warrants an exception to this policy, please contact us and explain your circumstances. We will review each case individually and may, at our sole discretion, offer accommodations.

Examples of circumstances we may consider:
